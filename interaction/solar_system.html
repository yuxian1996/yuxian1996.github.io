Solar system and solar terms simulation

Abstract

This is a interactive system introducing earth-moon system and Chinese 24 solar terms. For earth-moon system, we provide a general scene to experience days and nights on the earth. Users can use UI to jump to whenever they want and to one of four typical latitudes. For Chinese 24 solar terms, we provide 12 scene to experiece different spectacles in different terms.

Instruction

1) The game runs on windows platform. Decompress Solar_system.zip” and run solar_system.exe” under the folder.
2) Interaction
Use mouse to look around, hold and release space to select UI, use "wasd" and up/down/left/right arrow to interaction with UI. Press "u" to toggle UI.

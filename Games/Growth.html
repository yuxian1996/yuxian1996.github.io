Growth

Abstract

Growth is a third person puzzle adventure game where you play as an alien attempting to escape from area 51. Using space magic you alter the properties of objects around you as you reach the safety of your ship.

Use WASD to move your character, aim using the mouse, left click to fire your grappling hook, Q to move an object to you and E to push yourself off of it.
